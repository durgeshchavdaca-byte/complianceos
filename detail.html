ComplianceOS India — GST Registration

Overview

GST Registration is mandatory for businesses exceeding turnover thresholds. It ensures compliance with the Central Goods and Services Tax Act.

Why this applies

Your business turnover is above ₹20 lakh, triggering GST registration requirements.

Legal Basis

Central Goods and Services Tax Act, Section X. Authority: CBIC. Portal: gst.gov.in

Required Actions

  • File GST REG-01 form within 30 days of threshold breach
  • Maintain invoices with GSTIN

Deadlines & Triggers

Registration must be completed within 30 days of crossing turnover threshold.

Consequences if missed

Penalties, interest, and tax liabilities for late registration.

Corrective Route

Apply immediately with GST REG-01 and pay applicable penalties.

Source References

Notification No. 10/2024 — CBIC, July 2024.
